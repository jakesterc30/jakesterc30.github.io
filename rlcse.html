Rocket League

by Jacob Cano

Posted on December 13

Cars and soccer, two very different things combined into one action-packed, fun game. Rocket League is the newest, and fastest growing mmo-soccer game out there.

Rocket League is one of the most entertaining game there is right now. It's fast, it's fun, and it has rocket powered cars. Matches are 5 minutes long and you will always want to play another one.

Rocket League's gameplay is solid. I prefer to use a controller, because in my opinion it makes driving MUCH easier when you are able to rotate a joystick 360 degrees than use 4 keys to maneuver.

You have the potential to pull off absolutely crazy moves, you can fly through the air to carry the ball into the net or absolutely break a car's axels with your creative ball hits and angels. Watching people who are actually good at the game(spoiler: not me) play is a blessing. It is so much fun to see all of the crazy shit they pull off, most of which I didn't even believe was possible.

This game has hours of fun with it's many unique game modes. It has hockey AND basketball availible for play, as well as many different maps and slightly game altering game modes, IE using a beachball instead of a soccer ball or infinite boost. However, there is a large learning curve as it can be a bit difficult to get the hang of the game. 7/10
